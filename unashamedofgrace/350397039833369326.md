Boy you can certainly see I am an amateur to this blogdom stuff! OK, as promised, a short account of my salvation testimony. I grew up in a home where I was taken to church far before I ever wanted to be! Yet it meant basically nothing to me. At 12, I "joined the church" (SBC) mainly because I wanted to take the communion elemnets along with some of my friends! But at 16 I bought my 1st car, a 1965 Impala SS! It quickly became my idol. Not long afterward, though, my "idol" got a dent from a fender-bender that was not my fault. I got a huge empty feeling of, "Is this all there is to life?" Not long afterward, from somewhere in the recesses of my mind part of what I later came to realize was part of Mt. 7:23 crossed my mind. "Depart from Me..." I realized that would be what I heard from Jesus one day & I just simply froze with fear. I got on my knees & cried out to the Lord, but still did not understand the truth of salvation. Later I talked with my Dad, one of the greatest believers I have ever known, & he shared the truth of Christ to me. I don't know exactly when I believed in Him alone for eternal life, but the Lord used that ol' Impala to show me my need of Him, & I believe in Him today with all my heart! OK, let's hear from some of you!
