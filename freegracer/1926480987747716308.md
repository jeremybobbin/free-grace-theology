Welcome to Free Grace Theology Blog! I am glad that you are visiting! Free Grace Theology emphasizes the glory of God revealed through: the absolute freeness of eternal life, certain assurance of eternal life through faith alone in the objective promises of Christ, eternal security, the costliness of and the significance and meaning (the abundant life) through discipleship, the responsibilities of the child of God, the accountability of the Christian both in time and eternity, and the conditional glories, honors, and privileges for the overcoming Christian at the coming reign of Christ in the Kingdom of God.
